Welcome to Park Cities Presbyterian Church

Preschool Sunday School volunteers needed

As we enter into another year of Sunday School for our Preschoolers, we are in great need of classroom volunteers. Commit to a year of teaching our little ones by serving once-a-month or more downstairs on Sunday mornings.

 Details

Promotion Sunday

Sunday, August 25

Children’s Ministry begins the new calendar year, promoting all children to their new Sunday School classes. Detailed information about classrooms and teachers will be sent to parents the week before Promotion Sunday.

 Details

Old-Fashioned Hymn Sing

Sunday, September 1

Do you love the old hymns? Then don't miss this hour as we sing hymns joyously to the Lord. Everyone is invited.

 Details

Justice & Mercy Symposium

September 6–7

A biblical discussion of sex trafficking and domestic violence, prison outreach, race reconciliation, and neighborhood restoration and an Eyes-On bus tour the next day

 Details

Helping Hands event

Saturday, September 7

Be the hands and feet of Jesus and join the deacons and other volunteers to help single moms, widows/ers, senior adults, and those with chronic illness by doing odd jobs around their homes. Volunteers are always welcome and appreciated!

 Details

Children's and Youth choirs

September 8–11

All students, kindergarten through grade 12, are invited to grow in understanding more about music, singing, and worship by participating in a choir this fall. First rehearsals are Sunday, September 8 (high school) and Wednesday, September 11 (grades K-8). Preregistration required for K-1 due to limited enrollment.

 Details

PCPC Women's Fall Premiere

Wednesday, September 11

Don't miss our second annual gathering at the Angelika Theater! We will be looking at what it means to put aside our fears and rest steadily on the solid foundation of Christ. Tickets are $10. 

 Details

Union

September 12–October 30

Marriage is a union, not simply two people making a life commitment to each other, but more importantly, it is a couple making a covenant before a holy God who binds the union together. Because our goal at PCPC is not just to perform your wedding ceremony but to guide you into a marriage as God intended it, we encourage you to enroll in either the fall or spring Union course. Special note: The fall class begins Thursday, September 12, but will meet Wednesday nights subsequently.
